How To Play

Objective

Beat the dealer's hand without going over 21. Face cards are worth 10, Aces are 1 or 11 (whichever helps your hand most), and all other cards are worth their number.

Actions

  • Hit — take another card. Available whenever your hand is under 21.
  • Stand — end your turn and keep your current total.
  • Double Down — double your bet, take exactly one more card, then stand. Only on your first two cards.
  • Split — if your first two cards are a pair, split them into two hands, each with its own bet. One re-split allowed (max 2 hands). Doubling after a split is allowed.
  • Insurance — offered only when the dealer shows an Ace. Costs half your bet and pays 2:1 if the dealer has blackjack.

Dealer Rules

The dealer hits until reaching 17 or more, and stands on soft 17.
